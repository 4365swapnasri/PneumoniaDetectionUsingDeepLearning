About Us

Pneumonia Detection Using Chest X-rays is a machine learning project focused on improving pneumonia diagnosis through automated analysis of chest X-ray images. Pneumonia is a serious lung infection that requires timely and accurate diagnosis. Our system uses deep learning, particularly Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs), to detect signs of pneumonia in X-rays. This project reflects our commitment to combining technology and healthcare to create impactful solutions. It is designed to support doctors, not replace them, by enhancing diagnostic accuracy and efficiency.
